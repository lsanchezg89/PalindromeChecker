Palindrome Checker

Welcome to Palindrome Checker!

A palindrome is an alphanumeric expression that spells the same way forward and backward, without considering punctuation, spacing, case or other symbols. Some examples of palindromes are:

  • My gym
  • No lemon, no melon
  • Step on no pets
  • Racecar
  • 12344544321
